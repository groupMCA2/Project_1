Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check the on-road price of a car?

Click the "Check On-Road Price" button on any car listing. Enter your location, and we'll calculate the final price including taxes and registration fees.

What's included in the on-road price?

The on-road price includes the ex-showroom price, RTO registration charges, insurance, and other applicable local taxes.

How do I compare different cars?

Use our comparison tool to select up to 3 cars and compare their specifications, features, and prices side by side.

Are the listed prices negotiable?

Prices for new cars are generally fixed by manufacturers. For used cars, you can negotiate with individual sellers or dealers.

How often are prices updated?

We update our prices daily to ensure you get the most current information available in the market.
